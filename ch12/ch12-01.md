- The standard forms of the equation of a circle with center $(0, 0)$:
    - an ellipse becomes a circle with its radius $r$ when $a = r$ and $c = 0$ (the ellipse's foci collapse to its center)
$$
    \dfrac{x^2}{r^2} + \dfrac{y^2}{r^2} = 1
$$
or more frequently
$$
    x^2 + y^2 = r^2.
$$
