### 11.7 Solving Systems with Inverses

- The identity matrix $I$
$$
I = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\
0 & 1 & \dots & 0 \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & 0 & \dots & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$
such that
$$ AI = IA = A $$
for the $n \times n$ matrix $A$.

- The matrix multipilicative properties:
$$ A A^{-1} I \\ A^{-1} A = I  $$
